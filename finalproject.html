Lily's Final Project

My final project in CSC 106 at URI.

To play the game:

  1. Click "Start"
  2. You will be flying through a swarm of aliens that have attacked earth.
  3. Your goal is to collect 7 gems and level up. You will then battle the final boss.
  4. Using the arrow up or down, move the pegasus across the screen to dodge the aliens coming towards.
  5. There are also clouds they like to hide behind so be careful. Let's see how long you can last.
